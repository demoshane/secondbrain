## What Was Built / Fixed

### GPAG-02: Reopen done items
- The `done-check` change handler was always calling `POST /actions/<id>/done` (which sets done=1) regardless of whether the checkbox was being checked or unchecked.
- Fix: handler now checks `cb.checked` — if false, calls `PUT /actions/<id>` with `{done: false}` to reopen the item.
- The `PUT /actions/<id>` route in api.py was extended to accept a `done` field (previously only handled `assignee_path`).

### GPAG-03: Single-click assignee picker
- The `openAssigneePicker()` function created a `<select>` element but never auto-opened its native dropdown, requiring a second click.
- Fix: after appending to the DOM, call `sel.showPicker()` inside a try/catch (graceful fallback to `focus()` for browsers without showPicker support).

### GPAG-03 (people filter): All people notes now visible
- The picker was reading `_allNotes.filter(n => n.type === 'people')` from the module-level cache. This cache could be stale or missing entries depending on when `loadNotes()` last ran.
- Fix: `openAssigneePicker()` is now `async` and fetches `GET /notes` directly before building the dropdown, ensuring all currently indexed people notes appear.
